# FIMpad Themes

Place your caret within in a config tag below and press Ctrl+Shift+G to change the colors in your text editor.



## The Beach

A lively day at the beach.

```
[[[{
fgColor:"#d8d8d8";
bgColor:"#384868";
caretColor:"#c800c8";
selectionColor:"#dd9500";
}]]]
```
